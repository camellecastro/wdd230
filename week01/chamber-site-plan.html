Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Site Name: Naguilian Chamber of Commerce

Reason: Naguilian Chamber of Commerce. This name represents my home town in La Union, Philippines which the chamber operates.

Site Purpose

The Naguilian Chamber of Commerce functions as the pulse of the local business community, serving as a vibrant hub for business synergy. We facilitate connections with local entrepreneurs through our user-friendly online directory, fostering collaboration and meaningful relationships. We inform about exclusive events and forums, ensuring business owners stay updated and never miss an opportunity to learn, share insights, and connect with like-minded professionals. We also provide information on advocacy efforts and legislative developments that impact businesses in Naguilian.

Scenarios

Scenario 1: New Business Owner Seeking Networking Opportunities

Visitor Question: "As a new business owner in Naguilian, how can I connect with other local entrepreneurs to expand my network and explore collaboration opportunities?"

Website Content Focus: Develop a dedicated section highlighting networking events, membership benefits, and the user-friendly online directory to facilitate connections within the local business community.

Scenario 2: Established Business Owner Looking for Resources

Visitor Question: "I've been in business for several years; where can I find resources and information to stay updated on market trends, regulatory changes, and business growth strategies in Naguilian?"

Website Content Focus: Feature a comprehensive Business Resources Center with up-to-date information on regulatory updates, market trends, business guides, and toolkits.

Scenario 3: Community Member Interested in Local Advocacy

Visitor Question: "How can I stay informed about advocacy efforts and legislative developments that affect businesses in Naguilian? Is there a platform for voicing concerns and participating in surveys?"

Website Content Focus: Create a dedicated section providing information on advocacy initiatives, legislative updates, and a user-friendly platform for community members to voice concerns and engage in surveys related to local business issues.

Style Guide

Color Schema

Blue represents trust, security, stability, peace, and calmness. It is the color often used by businesses and banks to create a sense of security and trust in the brand. This color is the no. 1 preferred color by both men and women. Blue implies psychological dependability, wisdom, loyalty, and reassurance. Shades of blue in a website translates good security and inspires trust.

#E7EFC5 #A3C4BC #BFD7B5 #F2E7C9

Primary Color: #E7EFC5 | Backgrounds and Headers

The primary color will be used for the background of the website to create a clean and welcoming visual appeal. It will also be applied to headers and section titles, providing a consistent and calming foundation for the site's content.

Secondary Color: #A3C4BC | Paragraphs and Subheadings

The secondary color will be applied to paragraphs and subheadings to maintain readability and visual hierarchy. This color brings a sense of sophistication and complements the primary color, creating a harmonious visual experience for visitors.

Accent Colors: #BFD7B5 and #F2E7C9 | Call-to-Action Buttons and Highlights

Accent color #BFD7B5 will be used for call-to-action buttons, encouraging user interaction and drawing attention to key actions on the website. Accent color #F2E7C9 will be used for highlights and subtle design elements, adding a touch of warmth and visual interest to specific areas without overwhelming the overall color scheme.

Typography

Bebas Neue is a favorite in the design-sphere. Its condensed and clean form makes it excellent to use for headings. Round and narrow typefaces offset nicely against each other. The contrast between Bebas and Montserrat makes for a tidy and contemporary font combination.

Font 1: BEBAS NEUE

Usage: Bebas Neue will be employed for headings, providing a bold and attention-grabbing style. With its strong, uppercase characters, Bebas Neue adds a touch of modernity and emphasis to key headings throughout the website.

Font 2: Montserrat

Usage: Montserrat will be utilized for subheadings and paragraphs, ensuring a clean and sophisticated look. Montserrat's versatility and readability make it an excellent choice for conveying information and maintaining a clear visual hierarchy in subheadings and body text.
